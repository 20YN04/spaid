# PRODUCT.md — Spaid

## Product purpose
Spaid is a B2B employee-wellness platform that matches working parents of neurodivergent children (ADHD, autism, anxiety/stress, dyslexia, dyscalculia, ages 5–18) to specialized Belgian psychologists. Activated by the employer, used privately by the parent.

## Register
**brand** — the public-facing pages (home, programma's, contact, legal) ARE the product to a first-time visitor. The auth-gated app surfaces are minor; the marketing surface carries the trust-building work.

## Users
- **Primary**: a working parent of a 5–18-year-old with a clinical diagnosis. Exhausted, time-poor, juggling school calls + work calls + dinner. Comes in via an internal HR email or word of mouth.
- **Secondary**: HR / people teams at Belgian corporates (DKV, Elias, the federal government). Want a benefit that retains people and lowers absenteeism. Decision lasts 30 minutes.
- **Tertiary**: psychologists in the network — see their slots, get booked.

## Brand voice
Three physical-object words: *calm, considered, Belgian.*

- A handwritten note from a grandparent who happens to be a clinician
- A pharmacy in Leuven with brass fittings and a wood counter
- A KU Leuven thesis cover from the 1980s that has held up

Not: a SaaS pitch deck, a startup landing page, a wellness-app gradient.

## Anti-references
- Reflex Stripe / Linear / Vercel restraint on cream
- Editorial-magazine serif italic + tracked uppercase labels on every section (saturated lane)
- "Hero metric template": big number + label + tiny supporting stat + gradient accent
- Wellness-app pastel mint + sans-serif + meditation-blob aesthetic
- Tech-bro dark mode + neon accent

## Strategic principles
1. **Help arrives before the burnout.** Tone is supportive, never urgent.
2. **Private from the employer.** Reassurance must be visible on every page that touches health data.
3. **The clinician is the product.** Psychologists are named, photographed, specialized — not anonymous.
4. **No wait list.** Slot booking is friction-free; the calendar IS the homepage of the app.
5. **Bilingual NL/FR.** Belgium-first; never English-only.

## Anti-goals
- Pretending to replace clinical care. Spaid is the front door, not the room.
- Selling parents emotional content. Selling employers data dashboards. (We sell access to a clinician.)
- Cluttering the parent surface with B2B procurement copy.
